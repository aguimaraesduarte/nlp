Jonny Mattock is a drummer and percussionist from Northampton who was a member of, or played with, Massive Attack, Spacemen 3,[1] Spiritualized,[2] The Perfect Disaster, Slipstream, Lupine Howl, Cranes, Baxter Dury, The Breeders, The Jazz Butcher, Honey Tongue, Josephine Wiggs Experience, Freelovebabies.[3]

He is currently a tutor at Northampton College, working with Music Practice and Music Production students.
