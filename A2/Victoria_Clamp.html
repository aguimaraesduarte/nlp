Victoria Clamp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Victoria Clamp is an American musician, best known as a vocalist in Tom Tom Club, led by Chris Frantz and Tina Weymouth.[1][2][3] She is also the lead singer of the band BlondeStreak.[4]
