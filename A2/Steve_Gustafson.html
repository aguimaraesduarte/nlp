Steven E. "Steve" Gustafson (born April 10, 1957) is the bass guitarist for the American alternative rock band 10,000 Maniacs. He and Dennis Drew are the only remaining founding members of the group.

In 2011 Gustafson performed on a new EP and a 30-city 30th anniversary tour.[1]
